Good Evening, Fellow Shooters

I am pleased to announce the Trap & Skeet Ranges are cleaned up and ready to open for the 2025 shooting season!

Opening day is Saturday April 26th

A couple of things to note for the 2025 Season:

  • Hours for the 2025 season will remain the same as years past:
  • Saturday 9am – 3pm
  • Sunday 9am – 1pm
  • Weeknight shoot coming late May — likely Tuesday evening
  • Closed the last Sunday of every month (airsoft events)

Membership & Fees:

  • General WSC memberships can be purchased at the skeet shack — CASH ONLY
  • Trap & Skeet Associate Membership, Shooters Cards, Single Rounds, and Day Fees can be purchased with Cash, Credit, or Debit

General WSC Membership Fees:

  • General Member New – $70.00
  • General Member Renewal – $60.00
  • General Member over 65 New – $45.00
  • General Member over 65 Renewal – $35.00
  • Note: If your membership has not been active for 2+ years, you must pay new member pricing.

Non‑Member & Non‑Associate Fees:

  • Day Fee – $10.00
  • Cost Per Round – $10.00
  • Under 18 Cost Per Round – $5.00
  • Shooters Card (10 rounds + 1 free) – $100.00

Trap & Skeet Associate Member Fees:

  • Associate Annual Membership – $75.00
  • Cost Per Round – $8.00
  • Shooters Card (10 rounds + 1 free) – $80.00

As a result of supplier cost increases, we have made the tough choice to increase the associate membership fee this year from $50.00 to $75.00 annually. This allows us to keep round prices stable while funding repairs, upgrades, and clay target purchases.

We ended last year strong with new members and increased participation. For 2025, we are excited to announce upcoming improvements:

  • A berm project to enclose the skeet and trap ranges and retire the aging curtain system
  • Voice release system for the trap field
  • Digitizing our waiver collection

We have also created new Facebook and Instagram pages to keep members updated. Please follow and share!

Thank you for your continued support! We look forward to seeing you on the ranges this season!
